EMBRC-ERIC Mission :

  • Support and deliver on new scientific discoveries and knowledge of marine organisms and ecosystems;
  • Promote the use of marine experimental models in mainstream science and raise the profile of marine biological sciences;
  • Improve the sustainable utilization of marine biological resources;
  • Promote the European blue bio-economy.

The services offered :

  • Access to a portfolio of research platforms, biological resources, analytical services and data;
  • Support of access to marine biological material, including genetic material, and advice and guidance on the use of marine bioresources;
  • Integrated workflows of high quality services for access to biological, analytical and data resources by deploying standards
  • Strengthening of the connection of science with industry
  • Training facilities and courses for researchers and technical personnel;
  • Collaboration with research infrastructures
  • Support their environmental policies and blue bioeconomy.
